Welcome to IJSE

With the mission of “contributing the most capable and reliable software engineers, equipped with profound knowledge in the latest technologies to meet the demands of the ever-evolving IT industry,” the Institute of Software Engineering (IJSE) proudly celebrates 15 years of excellence. Since our establishment in 2010, we have significantly strengthened Sri Lanka’s IT sector by producing over 8,500 highly skilled software engineers who now thrive in leading technology companies across the country. Recognized as one of Sri Lanka’s top IT education providers, IJSE focuses not only on technical expertise but also on cultivating the essential soft skills and professional values demanded by the modern tech industry, delivered through our industry-relevant Software Engineering programs.
